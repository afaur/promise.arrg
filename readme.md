## Pirates.arrg

You are the capin of a ship! Your loyal crew is desperate to find some treasure!

There are 4 challenge files that you should be completed in order. There are
more challenges, but they are revealed within the challenges.

1. `1.gretting.js`
1. `2.getMap.js`
1. `3.giveMap.js`
1. `5.treasure.js`

To start, open up `1.greeting.js` in your editor of choice for tips on how to
help your crew out!

To test your work you can run:

~~~
node 1.greeting.js
~~~
